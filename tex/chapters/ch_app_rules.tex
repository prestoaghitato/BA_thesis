\chapter{Rules Found}
\label{ch:app_rules}
\chapterintro{This appendix lists all rules found including the absolute values for all metrics and the p-values for all metrics.
A brief legend of the tables: Every row represents a rule.
The first and second column give a rule's antecedent and succedent respectively.
Columns 3--5 give a rule's absolute values for confidence, \noc, and duration (in that order).
Columns 6--8 give a rule's p-values, again for confidence, \noc, and duration (in that order).
The 9\textsuperscript{th} and last column gives the number of null sequences the rule was found in.
Rules are sorted alphabetically according to their antecedent, then sorted alphabetically according to their succedent.
Although this way it is difficult to look for e.g.\ rules with a high confidence, the alphabetical sorting allows an individual rule to be found easily.
Section \ref{sec:289} gives values that were obtained with a delay of \(\geq\)~289~\ms; Section \ref{sec:850} gives values obtained with a delay of \(\geq\)~850~\ms.}
